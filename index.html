VeloStudio

A free video editor that runs entirely in your browser.
Your footage never leaves your device.Every cut, effect and export happens on your own computer. Nothing is uploaded to any server — there is no server. Your videos stay yours.

Import video, audio and images, arrange them on a timeline, trim and split, add text and effects, then export to MP4, WebM, GIF or audio — all without an internet connection once the page is open.

Tip: for the fastest exports, open VeloStudio in Chrome or Edge. Other browsers still work, but may export video more slowly.
